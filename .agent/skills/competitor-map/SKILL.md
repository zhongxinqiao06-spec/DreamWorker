## Instructions

Build a competitor map with:

- Direct competitors.
- Indirect substitutes and manual workarounds.
- Positioning, pricing, channel, and feature differences.
- Evidence strength and missing evidence.
- Differentiation hypotheses.
- Questions that should be validated before building.

Separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations.
