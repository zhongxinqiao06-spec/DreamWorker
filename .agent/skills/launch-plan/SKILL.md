## Instructions

Create a launch plan with:

- Positioning and target segment.
- Landing page promise and proof points.
- Channel actions.
- Demo script or sales motion.
- Feedback loop and success signals.
- Risks and rollback criteria.

Keep the plan executable by one independent builder.
